# Strategic Roadmap 2024-2025: Thai Creative AI Dominance

## Vision
To become the definitive AI-powered backbone for the Thai creative industry, bridging the gap between local cultural nuances and global production standards.

## Phase 1: The Foundation (Q3 2024) - "Thai Context Engine"
*   **Objective:** Solve the "Lost in Translation" problem in current LLMs.
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   **Phaya Thai LLM Layer:** Fine-tuned model specializing in Thai creative slang, formal/informal registers, and "Khum-Phua-Phan" (cultural wordplay).
    *   **Thai Script Standardizer:** Automatic formatting for Thai TVC, Drama, and YouTube scripts.
    *   **Identity & Security:** ISO-compliant data handling for agency intellectual property.

## Phase 2: Pre-Production Catalyst (Q4 2024) - "Visualizing Ideas"
*   **Objective:** Reduce pre-production time by 70%.
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   **Siam-Diffusion Storyboarder:** AI image generation optimized for Thai faces, locations (Bangkok streetscapes, Thai houses), and aesthetics.
    *   **Smart Budgeter (THB):** Localized cost estimation based on Thai production market rates (crew, gear, talent).
    *   **Location Scout AI:** Database integration for Thai locations with lighting/weather prediction.

## Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion (Q1-Q2 2025) - "Agencies & Creators Hub"
*   **Objective:** Centralize the creative workflow.
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   **Creative Collaboration Suite:** Multi-user real-time editing with Thai-specific approval workflows.
    *   **Marketplace Integration:** API connections to Thai talent agencies and equipment rentals.
    *   **Global Export Module:** Automatic subtitle/dubbing prep and cultural sensitivity checks for international distribution.

## Phase 4: Intelligence & Optimization (Q3 2025+) - "The Global Standard"
*   **Objective:** Predictive success for Thai content.
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   **Thai Trend Radar:** Real-time analysis of Thai social media (Pantip, X, TikTok TH) to suggest viral hooks.
    *   **Predictive Performance:** AI scoring for script engagement based on historical Thai audience data.
